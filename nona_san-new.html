Character baselines/guidelines:
➜ Noëlla lives in the woods (she's a xweetok after all). The haunted woods were the only woods available as a place to live when I created her (many years ago!) and since then I have been willing to create a mashup of clouds and spooky trees. Purple and blue are two of my favorite colors and when combined they are like eye candy to me.
➜ Noëlla eats mainly acorns, nuts and berries, plus occasionelly mushrooms and other plants. She is a peaceful creature that does not harm others for no reason. This is probably the reason why usuls and xweetoks get along so well, just as with cybunnies, ixis, and many other foresty pets (but not so well with lupes, kougras or any predatory species). I am basing this on the default xweetok petpage information and pictures.
➜ Noëlla likes to travel and explore new lands because she is very curious and loves to learn new things (while still advising caution towards the unknown). She uses a magic portal -TO DO: figure out how it works and how she found it- to quickly go to amazing places far away (why not Purled's island?) and remain able to get back home as fast as may be needed. Therefore access to magic is required in this story, but I am not convinced about neopet's faerie magic and would prefer either a witchy encounter, or even maybe an older ancient kind of magic.
➜ I am not sure about the clothing situation, because I like my pets being animals and clothes are probably just limiting their movements and capacities in nature-like environnements such as a forest (which they live in). I might just get some more for the fun of playing with neopets customization, and not represent these in my characters pictures and stories. However, I do like little accessories such as jewelry and face props even if they might not be really practical (not wigs though, my pet are not humans).

Challenge #3 : WORLD BUILDING
  • ✔ Environnement
  • ✔ Ruling powers
  • ✔ Cultural norms

My base world is Neopia, maybe in an old time when most of the land is still covered by forests? With an abondance of fruits, berries and nuts.
➜"the Xweetok Forest Time": They were so many of them! And all thriving. (NOELLA DOES NOT REMEMBER THIS TIME)
Xweetoks are social creatures : thousands of xweetoks, all living in harmony with nearly unlimited resources (food, water, shelters and lively compagnonship, which they really enjoy).
Older xweetoks were very respected for their wisdom, and all the advices they gave while "guiding" the very large flocks. This enormous community was formed by smaller family clans established a few trees away from each other. Everyone was welcome and also encouraged to go explore outside their own nest, knowing that they would always have a place to return to whenever they would feel the need to.
The family bonds are very strong between xweetoks. They last all their life.

➜"Nowadays": Now eveything is different and there are deserts, swamps, and many cities crowded with a lot of people sometinmes waging wars against one another!
Today the xweetoks are scaterred all over Neopia, with some even living in the cities, but since they are small preyed on and rather timid creatures, most of them have retired out of the plains and deaper into either the lower levels of mountains or the few forests left.
Since there isn't one very large group of xwetoks anymore, but instead a multitude of tiny family cells everywhere, it has become more difficult and dangerous for xweetoks to survive. They don't have access to the ancestral knowledge of their elders like they used to and each one is basicly just responsable for themselves, unless they have created a family. Being extremely found of company, many have found companions that aren't of the same species. This offers a better protection against the numerous predators out there.

➜"Our story": Noëlla found or established her home in the haunted woods. She thought that since theese were mostly inhabited by dead (harmless?) things (and therefore scary for a lot people that wouldnot get into those woods), she would probably be quite safe (always keeping a vigilant eye on those scary werewolves, though).
There is not so much food in the haunted woods becaus e of the conditions the trees are in, so Noëlla spends a lot of time exploring beyond them and searching for new fruit trees and bushes. This is slowly leading her further and further away from their home tree where she lives with her partner Noulfe and their kit/cub (no name chosen yet). They are installed with a very close usul friend (Plume) who just recently gave birth to her baby (Luette), and there is a bunny (Maënna) living with them (or on/in the grounds below them more precisely). -plus also a future cloud jubjub, when I am done playing with the lab-
They get along perfectly within this little familial cell. Xweetoks are after all some kind of mix between squirrels (like usuls) cats/foxes (that tail!) and rabbits (cybunnies).
While Noëlla is away looking for food, Maënna and Plume are trying to revive the woods and give back more life (starting with some more leaves!) to the bushes and trees around where they live. They use potions, spells and all the magic they can find.
